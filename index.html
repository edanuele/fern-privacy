Fern — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

Fern is a manual-entry budget tracker for Android and iOS. It is built so that your financial information never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Fern has no account system, no sign-up, no ads, and no analytics or tracking. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data.

Where your data lives

Every transaction, account, budget, category, recurring bill, and savings goal you enter is stored only on your own device. Fern works fully offline and makes no network connections — there are no Fern servers that receive your information.

Exporting your data

Fern can create CSV exports and JSON backups, and can restore from a JSON backup. These actions happen entirely on your device, and a file is only ever shared or saved when you explicitly choose to do so through your device's own share options. Once a file leaves Fern, it is governed by wherever you send it (your file storage, email, messaging, etc.) — not by Fern.

Biometric / device lock

If you enable the optional app lock, Fern uses your device's built-in authentication (such as fingerprint or face unlock) to unlock the app. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system; Fern never sees, receives, or stores it.

Children

Fern does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
